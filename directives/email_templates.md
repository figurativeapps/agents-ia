## COPYWRITING RULES

### DO's:
1. **Personalize:** Always use company name, industry, location
2. **Be specific:** Mention concrete results or benefits
3. **Keep it short:** Maximum 150 words
4. **One CTA:** Single clear call-to-action
5. **Provide value:** Lead with what they gain, not what you sell

### DON'Ts:
1. **Generic templates:** Avoid "Dear Sir/Madam"
2. **Multiple asks:** Don't ask for meeting + call + demo
3. **Jargon:** Keep language simple and clear
4. **Long paragraphs:** Max 3 lines per paragraph
5. **Pushy tone:** No aggressive sales language

### A/B Testing Variables:
- Subject line length (short vs descriptive)
- Email timing (morning vs afternoon)
- CTA type (meeting vs resource download)
- Social proof presence (with/without testimonial)
